# Bootlegs

<span class="archive-status" data-status="shell">shell — entries pending</span>

A register of unofficial Smiths and Morrissey recordings: audience tapes, soundboard recordings, broadcast captures, and circulating compilations. **Metadata only.** No audio is stored in this repo, and no audio is linked from the repo unless the linked source has documented rights status.

<div class="archive-callout">
Bootleg records carry: title (where known), venue and date of the underlying performance, recording source (audience / soundboard / radio / TV), generation, run length, circulation history, lineage, and rights notes. PJLM's bootleg coverage is the primary authority for circulation lineage where it overlaps.
</div>
