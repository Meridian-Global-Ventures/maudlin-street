# About Maudlin Street

A Smiths/Morrissey research archive: lyric notes, discography, live history, media references, source citations, and curated reading paths through external resources. The archive is the descendant in spirit of the long-running fan archive *Passions Just Like Mine* — a faithful companion, not a republisher.

## What this site is

- A content-first archive: indexes, schemas, citations, attributions.
- A provenance aggregator. Every fact carries a source.
- A companion to a local archival reconstruction of `passionsjustlikemine.com`.

## What this site is not

- Not a republisher of lyric pages, interview transcripts, or full reviews. Short attributed excerpts only.
- Not a media host. Video and audio referenced by remote URL only; nothing embedded from this repo.
- Not authoritative on facts the local PJLM reconstruction covers.

## Source authority

The local reconstruction of archived `passionsjustlikemine.com` is the **definitive source of truth** for every fact, page, lyric, asset, and provenance record it covers.

Secondary sources — retired Smiths/Morrissey fan sites recovered from the Wayback Machine, external review collections, advisor recommendations, and any other external input — are **additive review material only.** They may supplement, never replace, PJLM-derived content.

Conflicts between a secondary source and PJLM are logged as **candidates for review**, never applied automatically. No PJLM-derived page, lyric, fact, asset, inventory record, or source-of-truth assertion is overwritten, normalized, superseded, or weakened without John's express authorization.

The first planned future ingestion effort is Wayback Machine recovery from retired Smiths/Morrissey lyric sources. That phase remains future work.
