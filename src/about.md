# About Maudlin Street

Maudlin Street is a static site for long-form Smiths/Morrissey research:
lyric notes, discography commentary, interview metadata, archived-source
provenance, and curated reading paths through external resources.

## What this site is

- A content-first publication: pages, schemas, citations.
- An aggregator of provenance, not a republisher of third-party
  content.
- A companion to a local archival reconstruction of the historic
  fan site `passionsjustlikemine.com`.

## What this site is not

- It is not a republication of lyric pages, interview transcripts, or
  reviews from external sources. Short attributed excerpts only, with
  citation.
- It is not a streaming or video host. Interview video is referenced by
  remote URL; nothing is embedded from this repo.
- It is not authoritative on facts the local PJLM reconstruction
  already covers. See "Source authority" below.

## Source authority

The local reconstruction of archived `passionsjustlikemine.com` is the
**definitive source of truth** for every fact, page, lyric, asset, and
provenance record it covers.

Secondary sources - retired Smiths/Morrissey fan sites recovered from
the Wayback Machine, external review collections, advisor
recommendations, and any other external input - are **additive review
material only.** They may supplement, never replace, PJLM-derived
content.

Conflicts between a secondary source and PJLM are logged as
**candidates for review,** never applied automatically. No
PJLM-derived page, lyric, fact, asset, inventory record, or
source-of-truth assertion is overwritten, normalized, superseded, or
weakened without John's express authorization.

The first planned future ingestion effort is Wayback Machine recovery
from retired Smiths/Morrissey lyric sources. That phase remains future
work.
